    Two sizes. Infinite possibilities.

    Now you can choose between an 11″ iPad Air and an all-new 13″ iPad Air,1 each with a gorgeous, high-resolution Liquid Retina display that brings everything you love to do to life. Both displays deliver a brilliant, responsive and colour-accurate viewing experience.

    M2. Flat-out flies.

    The incredibly fast M2 chip brings breakout performance to iPad Air. With a powerful CPU, GPU and Neural Engine, it’s nearly 50% faster than the previous generation. So you can easily plan your house remodel in Trimble SketchUp, play the latest triple-A games like Zenless Zone Zero or use AI-enabled motion tracking in Onform to fine-tune your golf swing. All with amazing efficiency for all-day battery life.3

    iPadOS and apps. Get up to something wonderful.

    iPadOS is designed to let you do all the things you love easily and powerfully from first touch to finishing touches. Use multiple apps at once, write in any text field, and navigate quickly and efficiently using just your finger, Apple Pencil or the Magic Keyboard trackpad.2

    Apple Pencil Pro. Engineered for
    limitless creativity.

    Apple Pencil sets the standard for how drawing, painting, handwriting and note-taking should feel — intuitive, precise and magical. All with pixel‑perfect precision, low latency, tilt sensitivity and support for palm rejection. The all-new Apple Pencil Pro adds even more capabilities to bring your ideas to life like never before.2

    Explore more Apple Pencil features

    Magic Keyboard. Type it up in a snap.

    Magic Keyboard delivers responsive typing for writing and emailing.2 And a built‑in trackpad brings the precision you need for tasks like editing a spreadsheet. Magic Keyboard also features a floating cantilever design that lets you attach it magnetically, then adjust it for the perfect viewing angle.

    Learn more about keyboards for iPad

    Cameras. Capture your imagination. And everything else.

    Landscape 12MP Ultra Wide front camera with support for Centre Stage

    12MP Wide back camera
    with Smart HDR photos and 4K video

    The advanced front and back cameras on iPad Air make it easy to snap a photo or shoot a 4K video, have a video call or scan a document.

    Connectivity. Wait-less wireless.

    Stay connected and productive wherever you are with superfast Wi‑Fi 6E or choose a fast 5G cellular connection when you’re away from Wi‑Fi.4

    Designed to make
    a difference.

    What matters to you matters to Apple too. From using more recycled materials that minimise environmental impact. To privacy protections that give you more control over your data. To creating built-in features that make iPad accessible to all.

    The new iPad Air has a durable design that uses 100% recycled aluminium in the enclosure.

    Privacy is a fundamental human right. Which is why we design our products and services to protect it.

    The best technology works for everyone. That’s why our products and services are inclusive by design.

    Two sizes.
    Infinite possibilities.

    New 13″ iPad Air. Plus wonderful.

    The expansive and perfectly portable 13″ iPad Air gives you even more room to work, learn and play. View multiple apps in Split View. Concept collaboratively in Freeform. See more of your timeline in iMovie. And view more participants in your video call.

    Stunningly advanced display.

    An anti-reflective screen coating and True Tone keep text sharp in all kinds of lighting conditions. And with high brightness and P3 wide colour, images look stunning. Technically speaking, spectacular.

    M2.
    Flat-out flies.

    Lickety chip. Faster by design.

    The Apple-designed M2 has a 15% faster CPU, 25% faster graphics and 50% more memory bandwidth than the previous generation. This phenomenal core performance powers all types of productive and creative tasks. Play graphics-intensive games, create complex beats, and design and render your 3D creations in apps like Trimble SketchUp with amazing speed.

    Advanced Neural Engine for advanced AI apps.

    The 40% faster Neural Engine makes iPad Air an incredibly powerful device for AI, accelerating machine learning in iPadOS features like Visual Look Up, Subject Lift and Live Text capture. And it enables AI-driven note-taking in Goodnotes 6, one-tap photo enhancement in Photomator and pro-quality shot tracking in Onform: Video Analysis App.

    iPadOS and apps.
    Get up
    to something
    wonderful.

    Do it all.

    All at once.

    Stage Manager lets you multitask in new ways by allowing you to overlap and resize windows to look the way you want, so you can easily navigate between them. You can also group apps for specific tasks or projects and arrange them in your ideal layout.

    Built‑in apps for all the essentials.

    iPad Air comes with powerful and capable apps designed to help you create, connect and get things done. Browse, edit and share your images and videos with Photos. Create dazzling presentations with Keynote, and add style with Apple Pencil Pro.2

    Amazing apps for any ask.

    Apps can transform iPad Air into almost anything you need it to be. And with over a million apps on the App Store designed just for iPad, you’ll find the right app for whatever you want to do.

    Apple Pencil Pro. Engineered for limitless creativity.

    Intuitive and precise creative control.

    Squeeze.

    Apple Pencil Pro can sense when you squeeze it and brings up a new palette, so you can quickly switch tools, line weights and colours.

    Barrel roll.

    A new gyroscope allows you to rotate Apple Pencil Pro for precise control of shaped pen and brush tools.

    Haptic feedback.

    A custom haptic engine delivers precise feedback that you can feel. When you squeeze or double‑tap, you’ll feel a light pulse confirming the action.

    Apple Pencil hover.

    Preview exactly where your Apple Pencil will touch down on your display, and see a virtual shadow of your tool. So you can write, sketch and illustrate with even greater precision.

    Double-tap.

    Quickly switch between tools, like a pen and eraser, with a double‑tap on Apple Pencil Pro.

    Ready when
    inspiration strikes.

    Apple Pencil Pro pairs, charges and stores magnetically on the side of iPad Air. And if you lose or misplace it, you can easily locate your Apple Pencil Pro in the Find My app.

    iPad Air works with Apple Pencil Pro and Apple Pencil (USB-C).
    Two great options for all kinds of creativity.

    New

    Cameras.
    Capture your imagination. And everything else.

    Landscape front camera.
    Stay front and centre.

    Now located on the long side of iPad Air, the 12MP Ultra Wide front-facing camera creates a perfect video-calling experience — especially when iPad Air is attached to a Magic Keyboard. And with Centre Stage, machine learning enables the camera to automatically adjust to keep you centred in the shot.

    Advanced back camera.
    Shoot for the stars.

    The 12MP Wide back camera captures vivid photos and amazing 4K videos. And the large, high-resolution Liquid Retina display lets you retouch images, edit your film and easily share them — all on iPad Air. You can also use the back camera to scan documents and then fill them out with Apple Pencil.

    Built-in mics and speakers.
    Sounds perfect.

    Record and play back rich audio with dual studio-quality mics and landscape stereo speakers. The 13″ iPad Air delivers even better sound quality than the 11″ iPad Air, with double the bass — great for getting immersed in your favourite song.

    Connectivity. Wait‑less wireless.

    Blazing-fast connections.

    • Wi-Fi 6E delivers incredibly fast wireless connectivity — over 2x faster than the previous generation. So you can stream, download and share at high speeds.4

    • 5G cellular capabilities let you stay connected to the fastest wireless networks when you’re on the go.4

    • Now exclusively eSIM, enabling a fast, convenient and secure way to activate your cellular data plan directly on iPad Air.5

      Learn more about cellular for iPad

    • Bluetooth 5.3 low-latency audio supports next-generation headsets and hearing aids that use Bluetooth LE.

    Connect to displays, drives and devices with USB-C.

    USB-C gives you fast, easy connections to your go-to accessories like external displays, docks, drives and cameras. Perfect for quick transfers of large photos and videos.2

    Designed to make a difference.

    Big steps towards a smaller footprint.

    iPad Air contains more recycled materials than ever:

    • 100% recycled aluminium in the durable enclosure.
    • 100% recycled rare earth elements in all iPad Air magnets.
    • 100% fibre-based packaging for iPad Air, with 100% recycled or responsibly sourced wood fibres.6
    Our stores, offices and data centres are already carbon neutral. We’ve committed to making our entire carbon footprint neutral by 2030.
    More on Apple and the Environment

    Privacy. That’s Apple.

    The Health app makes it easy for you to access your health and fitness information — and encrypts it on device and in iCloud so no one can view your data without your permission.

    Passkeys eliminate the need for passwords on apps and websites. Just use Touch ID or Face ID to authenticate. Simple and secure.

    Safari Private Browsing locks browsing windows when they’re not being used, blocks known trackers from loading and removes identifying tracking data added to URLs as you browse.

    More on Apple and Privacy

    iPad for all.

    If you’re at risk of losing your voice, Personal Voice lets you create a voice that sounds like you, so you’ll be able to communicate with it using Live Speech for calls and conversations.

    Assistive Access offers users a distinctive interface with high-contrast buttons and large text labels, as well as tools to help individuals and trusted supporters adjust the experience in specific apps.

    VoiceOver gives audible descriptions of what’s on your screen — from battery level to who’s calling to which app your finger is on. You can also adjust the speaking rate and pitch to suit your needs.
